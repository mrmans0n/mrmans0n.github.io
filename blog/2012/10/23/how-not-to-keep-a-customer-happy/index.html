How Not to Keep a Customer Happy

Today the iPad mini was announced, as was announced by the media some months ago. But also a 4th gen. iPad was announced.

The 3rd gen. iPad was released 7 months ago , and now it’s outdated – a 600€ piece of crap. I wonder how the customers feel now.

But if you add to that mixture that iOS 6 deprecated the 1st iPad , with only a year and a few months of life…

I’m a happy owner of an iPad 2 but I’m starting to feel a little bit worried of how Apple treats us customers with this crazy deprecating policy.
